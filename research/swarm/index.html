Preliminaries

The vehicles that make up the swarm are called agents. Each agent will have its own set of information and values; the superscript on these values is the agent index. The physical space over which the swarm will be spread is partitioned into different bins. For the sake of ease in computation, we will restrict our attention to a 2D physical space (i.e, all our agents will stay on the ground). The desired final formation is a probability vector of dimension . Each element represents the swarm density in the corresponding bin . Of the bins, there are recurrent bins, which are bins that have a nonzero portion of the swarm inside them. The rest of the bins are called transient bins.
