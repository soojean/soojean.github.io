SooJean Han

Welcome to my personal website.

I am an assistant professor at KAIST School of Electrical Engineering, and the director of the Autonomous Control for Stochastic Systems (ACSS) Lab. My research interests lie broadly at the intersection of control theory and artificial intelligence. Some applications we study are stochastic systems, distributed sensing, and decision-making networks.

My research lab and I are graciously supported by the AFOSR AOARD, NRF, ADD, ETRI, and KIAST. We are also working in close collaboration with various faculty and researchers throughout Korea (e.g., KAIST, Seoul National University and Kyung Hee University) and the USA (e.g., UIUC). I serve as an Associate Editor for the IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters (RA-L).
