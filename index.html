Hello and welcome to my site!

I am currently beginning my first year as a PhD student in the Control and Dynamical Systems department at Caltech. I start officially in Fall 2017, but I am here early for summer research and am working with Professor John Doyle and Professor Soon-Jo Chung.

I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at UC Berkeley in Fall 2016. During the semester of Spring 2017, I was a part of Professor Claire Tomlin's Hybrid Systems Laboratory and I worked on developing FaSTrack (a modular framework for fast, efficient, and safe autonomous system trajectory planning) with her students Mo Chen and Sylvia Herbert. Recently, our paper was accepted to the 56th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control.
