Research Associate / Internships

  • Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL)

    (June 2018 - August 2018)

    I spent my summer of 2018 as an intern at JPL, where I was a part of Team CoSTAR, working on the DARPA Subterranean (SubT) Challenge under the supervision of Dr. Ali Agha. I implemented ORBSLAM using C/C++ and deployed it on our robotic agents to perform distributed collaborative mapping and merging using keypoint feature detection.

  • Caltech Center for Autonomous Systems and Technologies (CAST Lab)

    (June 2017 - August 2017)

    Prior to commencing my PhD officially in Fall 2017, I worked with Professor Soon-Jo Chung and postdoctoral scholar Dr. Kyunam Kim on supervising two undergraduate students participating in the SURF program. I used ROS and the VICON motion capturing system for distributed control over multiple Crazyflie quadrotors and have them perform coordinated stunts. Our PID and nonlinear controllers were implemented in Python and C++.

  • Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR Lab)

    (February 2017 - May 2017)

    I was a part of Professor Claire Tomlin's Hybrid Systems Laboratory (a subdivision of BAIR). With her students Mo Chen and Sylvia Herbert, I worked on developing the obstacle detection algorithm for FaSTrack (a modular framework for fast, efficient, and safe autonomous system trajectory planning) and used MATLAB to implement it. Our paper was accepted to the 56th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control.
