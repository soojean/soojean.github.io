Research Experience

  • Caltech Center for Autonomous Systems and Technologies (CAST Lab)

    (June 2017 - August 2017)

    Prior to commencing my PhD officially in Fall 2017, I worked with Professor Soon-Jo Chung and his postdoctoral scholar Dr. Kyunam Kim on supervising undergraduate students participating in the SURF program. I focused on a project using the ROS and VICON motion capturing system for distributed control over multiple Crazyflie quadrotors and have them perform coordinated stunts.

  • Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR Lab)

    (February 2017 - May 2017)

    I was a part of Professor Claire Tomlin's Hybrid Systems Laboratory (a subdivision of BAIR). With her students Mo Chen and Sylvia Herbert, I worked on developing the obstacle detection algorithm for FaSTrack, a modular framework for fast, efficient, and safe autonomous system trajectory planning. Our paper was accepted to the 56th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control.
